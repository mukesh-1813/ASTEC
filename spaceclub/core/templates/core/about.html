About ASTEC

Discover the mission, vision, and team behind the official Space Club.

Our Story

Exploring the Cosmos Since Inception

ASTEC Space Club is a community of passionate astronomers, students, and space enthusiasts dedicated to unraveling the mysteries of the universe. We host regular stargazing events, publish detailed astronomy magazines, and foster a collaborative environment for learning.

Our Mission

To inspire curiosity about the cosmos, provide educational resources on astronomy, and create a welcoming space for anyone fascinated by the stars.

What We Do

From theoretical astrophysics to practical telescope handling, ASTEC covers all spectrums of astronomy.

Observation Nights

Regular stargazing events using professional grade telescopes to observe planets, nebulae, and deep sky objects.

Magazine Publications

Monthly curated magazines featuring member articles, astronomical events, and recent discoveries in space science.

Workshops & Seminars

Educational sessions covering rocket science, theoretical physics, astrophotography, and telescope making.
